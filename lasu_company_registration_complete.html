LASU Internship Platform — Company Partnership Commitment

Effective Date: Upon Form Submission

This agreement establishes the terms under which your organization participates in the Lumintern‑powered internship ecosystem.

1. Commitment to Equitable Access

Your organization commits to accepting intern candidates across three development levels:

  • Type A (Skilled/Technical): CPT Level 3+ students ready for technical contribution.
  • Type B (Developing/Early Career): CPT Level 1-2 students with strong growth potential.
  • Type C (Alternative Track): Non‑coding technical roles (Design, IT Admin, Marketing, etc.).

2. Slot Distribution & Matching

Our Weighted Sum Model will present matched candidates based on role requirements, skills, and competency scores.

3. Your Authority & Responsibilities

You retain full interview & selection authority. You commit to structured supervision, feedback, and fair treatment for all interns.

4. Benefits to Your Organization

  • Access to pre‑screened, skill‑aligned talent
  • Reduced recruitment friction
  • Recognition as an equitable development partner

5. Agreement Duration

Valid for current academic session; either party may terminate with 30 days notice. Any students already placed at the time of termination will complete their internship term unless otherwise mutually agreed.

You must accept the Partnership Agreement to continue.

⚠️ Important

If your organization cannot commit to interns across all three development levels, you will not be able to participate. This ensures equitable opportunity for all LASU students.
